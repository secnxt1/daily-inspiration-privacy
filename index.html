Privacy Policy for Daily Inspiration

We do not collect analytics or device data. No tracking technologies are used within the App.

The App does not use third‑party SDKs, analytics services, or advertising networks.

Because the App does not collect user data, no information is stored or shared. Your use of the App is fully local to your device.
